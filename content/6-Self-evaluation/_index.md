My three-month internship at **Amazon Web Services Vietnam Co., Ltd. (AWS Vietnam)** — spanning from **April 20, 2026** to **July 15, 2026** — was a defining milestone that bridged theoretical knowledge with hands-on cloud engineering practice.

Throughout this period, I contributed to **designing and deploying a high-availability fashion retail web portal integrated with an automated machine learning sales-forecasting pipeline on AWS**. This work deepened my expertise across multiple domains: **cloud infrastructure architecture, relational database administration (RDS PostgreSQL), serverless application development (Lambda & API Gateway), large-scale data engineering (AWS Glue & PySpark), and machine learning operations (MLOps)**.

Beyond technical output, I maintained a strong professional attitude — consistently meeting deadlines, respecting workplace standards, and actively contributing to team discussions to drive better outcomes.

### Standout Performance Highlight

* **Collaborative Effectiveness — Good:** Effective team leadership is less about issuing directives and more about cultivating psychological safety. I prioritized building an environment where every member felt empowered to raise questions or challenge assumptions. When teammates faced blockers, I chose to work through the problem alongside them rather than simply providing answers — fostering shared ownership and collective skill development.

### Areas Identified for Further Development

* Strengthen communication and storytelling skills to convey complex cloud architecture decisions and data modeling concepts in concise, business-friendly terms for non-technical stakeholders.
* Build greater composure and decisiveness when troubleshooting high-severity incidents, particularly during live production rollouts or infrastructure failover drills.
* Invest further in engineering best practices — including systematic unit testing strategies and mature CI/CD pipeline scripting — to elevate overall delivery quality.
