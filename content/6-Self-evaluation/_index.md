Throughout my internship at **Amazon Web Services Vietnam Co., Ltd. (AWS Vietnam)** from **April 20, 2026** to **July 15, 2026**, I was fortunate to step beyond the classroom and immerse myself in a professional cloud-computing environment where theoretical knowledge was put to the test every day.  
My core contribution focused on **designing and deploying a fault-tolerant fashion e-commerce platform integrated with an automated MLOps forecasting pipeline on AWS**, which sharpened my abilities in **cloud-native architecture, relational database management (RDS PostgreSQL), event-driven serverless development (Lambda, API Gateway), large-scale data processing (AWS Glue & PySpark), and end-to-end machine learning operations**.  

Throughout the internship, I maintained a strong sense of ownership over my tasks, adhered to team agreements, and consistently sought feedback to refine my deliverables.

### Areas for Improvement

* Strengthen the ability to translate complex technical architectures into concise, non-technical narratives for business stakeholders — particularly when presenting infrastructure cost trade-offs or ML model limitations.
* Build greater confidence and structured thinking when debugging distributed systems under time pressure, such as tracing data pipeline failures across multiple AWS services simultaneously.
* Develop a more disciplined habit of writing automated test suites and infrastructure-as-code documentation from the outset of a project, rather than retrofitting them at the end.
