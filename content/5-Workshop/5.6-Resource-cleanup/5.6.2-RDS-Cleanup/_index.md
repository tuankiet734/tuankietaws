### 5.6.2. Deleting Amazon RDS Database Instances

1. Go to the **Amazon RDS Console** and click **Databases**.
2. Select the database instances `fashion-rds` and `training-db`.
3. Click **Actions** -> Select **Delete**.
4. Uncheck the *Create final snapshot* option, check the acknowledgment box, and click **Delete** to tear down the databases.
